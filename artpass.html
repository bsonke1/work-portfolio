Local Arts Events Hub Mobile App and Website for Event-Lovers

Problem

Users don’t often attend local arts events because they don’t have a streamlined tool to find events that suit their interests, making staying at home more convenient. Users enjoy going out with loved ones, but aren’t aware of events to which they can invite friends.

Solution

A mobile app and desktop website that helps users search for events, suggests events for users based on their personal interests, and allows them to share interesting events with loved ones. A paired “rewards” feature to give users incentive to attend more events.

Team

UX Research, UX/UI Design, Front-End Development

My Roles

UX Research, UX/UI Design, Front-End Development

Timeline

4 weeks

Summary

ARTPASS, a conceptual design project, promotes greater audience numbers at local arts events through a seamless, one-stop event search application paired with rewards incentives.

This 4-week project consisted of:

  • UX Research, including competitive analysis, a user survey, and user interviews
  • Wireframe and Mockup Design
  • Usability Testing
  • Visual Design, including color, typography, and logo design
  • Front-End Development (HTML, CSS, light Javascript)

I participated in each step of the project from research to design to development. I used strengths in empathy, organization, time management, and “big picture” thinking to work with my team, ensuring everyone used their favorite strengths and that we hit our deadlines on time.
