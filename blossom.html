Gamified Plant Care Coach Mobile App for Innocent Plant Neglectors

Problem

Users who own potted plants have a lack of time, knowledge, and reminders to care for their plants, which hinders their plants' health.

Solution

A mobile app that reminds and educates users about plant care and archives necessary plant care schedules using a fun & interactive “Tamagotchi” inspired interface.

Team

UX Researcher, UX/UI/Product Designer

My Roles

UX Researcher, UX/UI/Product Designer

Timeline

6 weeks

Summary

My team's goal was to find an effective solution to house plant neglect because we ourselves appreciated the benefits of plants, but weren’t the best at keeping them healthy.

We created an app that features a “plant care coach”, external reminders and an easy-access, archived database of plant care info. We also included a fun, interactive interface to elevate the user experience.

I added a Visual Design layer to the design, including color palette, typography, and logo, and vector illustrations, to the interface for an added layer of delight.
