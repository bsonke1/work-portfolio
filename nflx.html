Rapid Netflix Desktop Prototype for Student Usability Test Practice

Problem

Students needed a website for usability testing for content launching in 1 week.

Solution

A testable, low fidelity prototype with which students can practice usability testing.

Impact

Prototype was present in time for product launch. 30+ students are able to successfully practice usability testing on course schedule.

Team

UX/UI Subject Matter Experts, UX Writer

My Roles

UX Designer, Curriculum Product Designer

Timeline

1 week (within a larger project)

Summary

One week before curriculum content was released, our team realized students had no way of conductingthe usability test assigned in their homework. As the resident Figma expert on the team, I had a week to make a Netflix prototype before the product launched.

Using a design system and Figma features for efficiency, I created a rapid prototype with a user-relevant feature that could be tested for usability.
